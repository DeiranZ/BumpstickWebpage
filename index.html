A Fresh Platformer

Bumpstick is a unique platformer, where you control a bouncy neon stick and move through levels as fast as possible.

Visit the Steam page

Features Overview

Unique Gameplay

Take control of your neon stick and bounce through the levels as fast as you can!

  • - fight for the best time in online leaderboards
  • - unlock new customization options for your Bumpstick
  • - save your favorite replays

Multiplayer

Bumpstick supports local multiplayer for as many people, as you can fit in front of the screen!

  • - set up the looks of each player
  • - fight for the first place
  • - play in special PvP game modes

Level Editor

Have a cool idea for a level? You can create it using the same editor that was used to create all the levels in the game and post it to the Steam Workshop, so others can play it too!

  • - play on levels created by other players
  • - create your own ones and share them
